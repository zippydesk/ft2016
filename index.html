--- title: Front-Trends 2016 layout: splash ---

Front-Trends is one of Europe’s leading front-end conferences. For 3 full days, web and mobile developers can discover the latest trends, technologies, and inspirations in front-end development. Since 2010, Front-Trends has invited a line-up of global speakers who shape face of the internet and the future of human-technology interaction.

Front-Trends 2016 is held on May 18-20, 2016 in Warsaw, Poland.

Topics range from the inspirational, technical, and exciting, to experimental, live-coding, interactive sessions—our speakers take the audience through many different areas of front-end.
