00ps!

Looks like something didn’t work as expected and your payment didn’t get through. If you know what might have gone wrong, go to the registration page and try again. Depending on the payment method you chose, a problem could be laying on the payment provider’s side.

You can also try processing the payment again by clicking Pay online on the Proforma invoice you received by e-mail.

If, however, you think the problem is on our side, please email us at tickets@front-trends.com with the following information:

  • When did the problem occur? What screen were you on that led you to this site?
  • Did you see any error message or code? Do you remember what it said?
  • If the paymant was with a PayPal account: do you have enough funds?
  • If the payment was with a credit card: did you provide all data associated with the card you tried paying with?
