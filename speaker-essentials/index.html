Dear Front-Trends Speaker,

Please take a moment to read this page carefully, as it contains information regarding transportation, scheduling, and presentation requirements at the event.

What We Offer

  • We cover the cost of your flight, hotel, and transportation while in Warsaw.

Event overview

Speaker Dinner before the event.

Three full days, May 18-20 (W, Th, F) on a single track. 6-8 talks per day. Lightning talks and discussion panels are also placed throughout the schedule depending on interest.

The audience is comprised mainly of intermediate-to-advanced web developers working in JavaScript and UI developers (HTML, CSS), front-end agencies, UX and UI designers, front-end team leads, etc. from all over the world.

Conducted in English. Keep up to date with the schedule for exact time slots (as soon as we publish it).

Flight & Transport from/to the Airport

Front-Trends covers the cost of your rountrip flight or train.

Upon your arrival, look for someone with the Front-Trends logo. Our staff will be looking out for you at the airport or station to send you safely to the hotel via taxi. If for unexpected reasons, there is no one there to assist you, please take only taxis parked directly in front of the airport exit with a taxi sign and save a receipt for refund.

Accommodation/Hotel

You’ll be staying at InterContinental, a 4-star hotel in the heart of Warsaw with a beautiful view, spacious rooms, and the highest publicly available swimming pool in Europe.

The hotel is 20–40 minutes away from the airport, and 15–30 minutes away from the venue, depending on traffic.

Conference Transportation

We organize taxi service during your stay. You’ll be picked up from InterContinental between 8:00–8:45 each day. Cabs will also be available at day’s end. On Monday, October 12, transportation will be organized to and from the party venue.

Speaker’s Dinner

We’d like to invite you to an exclusive dinner for speakers at 19:30 on Sunday, October 11. The dinner location is Kafe Zielony Niedźwiedź (address: Smolna 4, 00-375 Warsaw). Presentation

  • The main screen will be a 8 m × 4,5 m LED screen, 2752 × 1548 physical pixels (1920 px × 1080 px recommended slides resolution), with a VGA, HDMI and Display Port cable available on the stage.
  • You’ll have a headset microphone, a preview screen for your slides, an audio cable (mini-jack), and a stage sound monitor.
  • We strongly encourage you to send your presentation in advance for testing.
  • Let us know if you will perform live coding, or need access to sound or internet. We strongly suggest that you prepare screencast sequences as a backup plan for anything you’d like to show online during your talk.
  • Each slot is 30 minutes including Q&A. During the presentation, we will display the remaining time, and we can also notify you with a sound about the remaining time. Please plan accordingly.
  • We prepare two speakers at once during each break. If you are the second speaker in a set, please note that we will need to prep you during the preceding break (not right before your presentation), that includes setting up your laptop.
