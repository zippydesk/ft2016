Stuff You Can Do With Sponsorships

We’re pretty flexible with however you want your sponsorship to look, feel and work. From recruiting, to brand visibility, to web traffic/conversions and more - discover some of the things that your organization can do and achieve at Front-Trends.

  • Booth Space

    Best For: Recruiting, Marketing, Exhibiting Products, Networking

    Starts From: Gold

    Having your own company space is the best way to be a part of event itself, meeting attendees face-to-face and getting them in touch with your brand. Previous sponsors have offered video game stations, lounge hammocks, competitions, hackathons, testing devices and treats at their booths.

  • Online Visibility

    Best For: Leads, Traffic, Conversions, Visibility, Social Media Exposure

    Starts From: Silver

    Front-Trends offers all sponsors visibility on the website, Facebook posts, Twitter mentions and logos online. Sponsors can go a step further. Front-Trends Vimeo talks have been viewed over 130,000 times, offering a space for your video advertisements at the beginning or end of the recordings.

  • Food & Party

    Best For: Brand Loyalty, Social Media Exposure, Networking, Visibility

    Starts From: Gold

    The most important (but often overlooked) part of a conference is the FOOD, DRINK and NETWORKING quality of the event. Front-Trends always offers high-quality gourmet food, drinks and party venues. Sponsoring gives you a chance to host any one of the many options: an exclusive speakers’ dinner, the conference party, party drinks, or a simple lunch or breakfast. Video recordings and a photographer are present and you’ll receive the digital media package of your sponsored meal or party.

  • Swag and Digital Goods

    Best For: Brand Loyalty, Visibility, Social Media Exposure, Leads

    Starts From: Silver

    We encourage all sponsors to give out stuff to our attendees, but non-essentials can lead to conference-clutter. Sponsor swag in a practical, long-lasting way, by putting your logo on Front-Trends Tote Bags, Lanyards, Badges, Mugs, Travel Gear or Electronics. You can also offer digital vouchers, flyers, bring or ship your company banners, offer T-shirts, and much much more.

  • Live Event Visibility

    Best For: Recruiting, Brand Loyalty, Visibility, Social Media Exposure, Traffic

    Starts From: Silver

    We make sure our sponsors are always present, whether it’s your video or logo on the main stage screen, or part of our photo-walls (both digital and physical). We take care of your standing banner visibility and any other logistics that get you in the perfect spot to maximize attendee traffic.

  • Speaker Talk

    Best For: Visibility, Social Media Exposure, Traffic

    Starts From: Silver (lightning talk), Gold (main talk)

    Your sponsorship can also lead to the stage for a main talk (30 min) or a lightning talk (5-10 min). We’re adamant that every Front-Trends talk is filled with valuable content, not pitchy marketing talks. However, the web and mobile has many companies and employees that can share their valuable work, and a sponsored talk is the perfect way to get your organization recognized while blowing the minds of the Front-Trends audience.

Pricing Levels and Benefits

Whether you’re a smaller business or a large company, we’ve created pricing levels that have worked year after year with our partners. While the pricing tiers aren’t negotiable, everything beyond that point is fully customizable.

  • Startup Partner €1000

    • Digital and printed listings on the website and printed materials
    • 1 ticket to conference

  • Silver Sponsor €2500

    • Digital and printed listings on the website, videos, exclusive social media mentions, and display advertising at event
    • Silver-Level sponsorship opportunity (e.g. Swag, Food, Drinks, Booth negotiable)
    • 1 ticket to conference + up to 2 discounted tickets

  • Gold Sponsor €4000

    • Digital and printed listings on the website, videos, exclusive social media mentions, and display advertising at event
    • Gold-level sponsorship opportunity (e.g. Booth, Party, Talk)
    • 2 tickets to conference + 3 PR badges (access to all events except main hall)

  • Platinum Sponsor €7000

    • Top Front-Trends listing and exclusive video partner
    • Digital and printed listings on the website, videos, exclusive social media mentions, and display advertising at event
    • Main stage, 5-minute pitch after lunch break
    • World-level sponsorship opportunity (e.g. Booth, Party, Main Talk)
    • Social media mentions (Vimeo, Facebook, Twitter)
    • 4 tickets to conference, 2 tickets to exclusive speakers’-only dinner, 3 PR badges (access to all events except main hall)
